ania Markin

Born in a small village in western Russia to a severely depressed woman with four children (Alexei the oldest, twins Lyuba and Nikolai, and Ania the youngest). When Nikolai died their mother went crazy with grief. Nearly 2 years later their house burned down (most likely set on fire by mother), killing both parents as well as Lyuba. Alexei escaped with Ania and went west, eventually ending up in Lithuania and begging fare on a cargo ship to Denmark.

Her big round, dark eyes always make her look innocent and awe-struck by whatever's going on. She wears a little red and white kerchief like Sabah's and a traditional Russian dress that Elisabeth let out as she's gotten older (since she wore it from Russia), but she's grown so much now they are trying to copy the pattern to fit her as she grows more.

She learned German relatively quickly since she was young, but she still has a small accent or speech impediment.

She doesn't remember much about Russia or their family, but Alexei worries that she could develop depression or bipolar similar to him and their mother. She views Alexei as a guardian and likes to run up to him and give him a hug, or jump so he'll catch her. When Alexei fell apart, Sabah took her under her wing, so Ania sees her as a guardian and sister figure as well. She loves to go on piggy back rides and sit on people's shoulders, especially Alexei and Sabah (it makes her feel taller).

Strawberries are her favorite food- sometimes for shortcake, sometimes on bread in the morning, but always strawberries. Eliza always saves a jar of preserves for her name day on April 8, which she celebrates instead of her birthday.
