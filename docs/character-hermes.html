hermes

Hermes is one of the twelve Olympian gods and the herald of Olympus. He protects other heralds, travellers, thieves, merchants, and orators. Along with Thanatos and Gethos, he acts as a psychopomp, guiding the souls of the dead to the Underworld. He is the son of Zeus and Maia, and the father many minor gods including Pan and Hermaphroditus, as well as the young gods Minaria and Heredes.
