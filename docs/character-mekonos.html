mekonos

Mekonos is the son of the young gods Minaria and Gethos. He was born on his mother's island of Seezo and raised in his father's home in the Underworld. He and his twin sister, Akonitia, assist their father in dealing with souls that linger at the boundary of death. If the soul requires a painless death or a strong sedative, Mekonos provides.

From mêkôn (poppy). Unripe seed pods produce the basis for opiate drugs, which produce sedative effects. Sacred flower of Demeter, and common symbol of Hypnos, Thanatos, and Morpheus, because it represents eternal sleep.
