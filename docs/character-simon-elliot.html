simon Elliot

Short sandy hair, droopy hazel eyes (like a cow), freckles, autistic, sticking-out ears

Irish!

He often looks sad and forlorn, and stares off into space while thinking.

He talks very rarely, especially since he isn't a strong German speaker, so he mostly communicates with shrugs, facial expressions, and sighs. Johan often has to translate his body language, as well as the odd muddle of Danish and German that he has picked up. He'll talk more to Danny, Lana, and ELiza, who are native English speakers, as well as Maja and the Hübers since they can understand a bit from studying. He focuses (and communicates) best when his hands are distracted, so Johan taught him some knots to make with a piece of rope; otherwise he constantly clenches and unclenches his hands or picks at his skin. He also focuses well when sketching, which Charlotte has helped him practice.

He was largely illiterate at first, but Elisabeth has worked her magic and helped him improve by leaps and bounds.

He likes to play with wooden animal figures that Henry carves, especially a fox, a rabbit, and a badger, and often asks for specific animals on his birthday.

He mostly does farm-type chores similar to what he did growing up, but he's also good at small-motor tasks like preparing vegetables or setting fishing rods.
