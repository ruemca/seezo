hofer Else und Alfred

The Hofers run a farm near the village of Hafen auf Gemensa. They were close to Hans and his family, and helped Vera when he died. When Min and Henry left Seezo together, the Hofers helped them get back on their feet, and when they returned with the rest of the Seezo band, they let them stay for a few weeks to figure out what to do next. They have always been close to the Seezo community and acted as parental figures when the young people were struggling.
