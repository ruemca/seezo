Akonitia

Anokitia is the daughter of the young gods Minaria and Gethos. She was born on her mother's island of Seezo and raised in her father's home in the Underworld. She and her twin brother, Mekonos, assist their father in dealing with souls that linger at the boundary of death. If the soul requires a painful death or a strong medicine, Akonitia provides.

From akoniton (aconite, monkshood, or wolf's bane) flower. Highly toxic plant used to poison hunting arrows and kill prisoners. Touching or consuming the plant causes nausea, diarrhea, vomiting, burning, numbness, and motor fatigue. However, when handled carefully and taken in small doses, the plant can be a beneficial immune booster.
