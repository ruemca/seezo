emily Moore

She has chin-length stringy, straw-yellow hair with bangs, usually tied in a pony tail or half-up, framing keen green eyes. She likes wearing flowery, calico prints.

Born in a slum south of London to a Ernest and Rebecca Moore. Emily's brother, Lawrence, left to apprentice with a smith when he was about 10 years old, and accompanied him to Germany some time later. When Emily was 4 or 5, her parents sent her to live with Lawrence, but didn't tell her why. When she arrived at the address in Hamburg, however, there was no smith, and no Lawrence. An old woman helped her find the local blacksmith, where Rainer worked, who in turn sent her to Vera and Min. She never found her brother.

She's sweet and obedient, but still outgoing in a childlike way of asking more questions than the grown-ups feel comfortable with- she isn't afraid of honesty, but also usually understands when to keep quiet.

She reveres Eliza because she's the “older girl” who's also English. She always asks Eliza to make British pudding for her birthday because she remembers them from England. She lives with Vera because she's friends with Sonia and Susanna (otherwise she'd live nearer to Eliza).

Blue is her favorite color, and she wears it nearly every day (especially in her hair ribbons). When Susanna makes daisy crowns, she always adds forget-me-nots. She's best at bringing out Susanna and getting her to laugh and talk and play.

Secretly, she still hopes that Lawrence will come back and find her someday, but she's haunted by the possibilities of what might have happened to him.

She loves to play with the paper dolls Vera made for her for her birthday, but desperately wants a real rag doll. She's very good about sharing her dolls with everyone, and not playing with them during work time.

She likes to watch the fire burn and often sleeps near the fireplace in the colder months. Vera lets her help light the fire, but she's not allowed to tend it on her own just yet.
