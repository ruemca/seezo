friedrich III

King Friedrich III ascended the throne at age 29 when the previous king, his childless uncle, died. He had an arranged marriage to Italian duchess Silvia Rosario Noemi, three years previously, but she was infertile and died of heart failure. His second marriage, to Polish princess Izabella Maria, was also arranged, but they were very much in love with each other. She had one miscarriage, then died giving birth to Alexander after five years of marriage.
