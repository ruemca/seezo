joceline Poirier

Short, straight almost-black hair with bangs, deep-set brown eyes, pale skin, square face with slight features. Wears a yellow and orange flowered headband.

She lived in a slum on the outskirts of Paris until the Black Plague took her mother and brother, Olivier. Her mother wrote to her uncle Antoine when she first became ill, asking him to come and be the godfather. He took over Joceline's care of her mother and brother and wouldn't let her near them. When Olivier died, Antoine wrapped him up and sent away the body before she could see and she ran screaming after the carriage, but he caught her in his arms and she just beat on him. After that, they went to live at his home in Belgium. When he began to sexually abuse her, she ran away and wandered along the coast in Holland and Germany looking for people who spoke French. She befriended Charlotte immediately when she arrived at Seezo so they could speak French together.

She cleans herself obsessively, because when she took care of her sick family she was always afraid of catching the plague. She keeps everything very neat and tidy, and always cleans area around her bed in the morning. She prefers to do domestic chores with Lana and Eliza rather than work outside in the garden.

She loves cats and grooms all the barn cats, but scolds them when they bring her dead birds.

She sings traditional French songs and leads everyone in old Carols at Christmas time because it's her birthday. Charlotte instructed Eliza on how to make Busch de Noël for Joceline's birthday cake as well.
