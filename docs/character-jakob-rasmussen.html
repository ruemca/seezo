jakob Rasmussen

White blond hair slightly yellower than Annelie, thick but not curly, and a bit shaggy and choppy from the rudimentary haircuts they managed in the mountains; brown eyes, ruddy cheeks, black vest with faded yellow rosemaling and red scarf

Grew up with a band of fellow orphans in rural Denmark, surviving by stealing from surrounding villages. (brothers Amund and Terje, Max, Annelie, Jarl, Felix, Oskar, Viggo, Kaj, Hugo, and baby Jakob.) One of the older boys ended up committing murder, and the authorities were coming to break up the camp, so everyone scattered. Annelie fled south with Jakob and started stealing food again, but they were caught. Min heard about the orphans who were being locked up in jail and convinced the police to let her take care of them instead of burdening the state.

He was the youngest in the Mountain Band by far, often considered the “baby,” so Annelie sheltered him from most of the crime. He was very excited to meet boys close to his own age at Seezo and have real friends to play with. He can be nervous about trying new things, but he's generally willing to jump in head first. He's usually cheerful and has a lopsided grin from his baby teeth falling out. He's also naïve and doesn't get subtle meanings hidden in conversation.

He's a decent cook, since he wasn't allowed to help with robberies and therefore had to stay back and help make meals most days. He loves food, and when Eliza said she could make him a feast for his birthday, his eyes got huge- he thinks she must be a witch to make so much food at once.

He doesn't remember their family at all, so he regards Annelie as a mother figure as well as a sister. However, he's still curious about their parents and questions her incessantly. He loved listening to stories around the campfire in the mountains from Annelie and the other boys, and Elisabeth has used some of these stories to teach him to read and write.

He was fascinated with all the animals when he first got to Seezo, especially cats, since he'd never seen them so tame before.
