annelie Rasmussen

Curly white blonde hair contrasts and brings out stormy grey eyes, eyebrows so pale that you can hardly see them, but somehow she still manages to pull out a merciless glare; black jumper with faded yellow rosemaling

Grew up with a band of fellow orphans in rural Denmark, surviving by stealing from surrounding villages. (brothers Amund and Terje, Max, Annelie, Jarl, Felix, Oskar, Viggo, Kaj, Hugo, and baby Jakob.) One of the older boys ended up committing murder, and the authorities were coming to break up the camp, so everyone scattered. Annelie fled south with Jakob and started stealing food again, but they were caught. Min heard about the orphans who were being locked up in jail and convinced the police to let her take care of them instead of burdening the state.

She was used to standing up for herself in a crowd of adolescent boys, so coming to Seezo was a bit of a shock to her system. She didn't want to be taken care of by anyone else to have strangers touch Jakob, so she resisted and rebelled. For the first few weeks, she scowled and snarled silently, while Min and Maja quietly approached her a few times. So far, she refuses to talk about their lives before the mountain band, even to Jakob, though he asks incessantly. Min thinks getting her to talk about it will be important in getting her to trust them and adjust.

She's smart and calculating, and often plans things out carefully, but she can also attack blindly when caught off guard. She became quite skilled at stealing food, but she'd always had a den to run back to in the mountains- once she was just on the streets in unfamiliar cities, she was caught.

She often played the "decoy" in a heist, acting sweet and innocent to distract the victims of a theft. Even now, it can be hard to tell when she's faking niceties to get something or truly starting to soften up. She can also have mood swings sometimes, especially as a young adolescent- she started her period some time after coming to Seezo.

She doesn't know her real birthday and refused to pick one when they suggested it, so Min picked February 8, the day they arrived in Seezo. She got a black and yellow shawl to match her and Jakob's clothes.
