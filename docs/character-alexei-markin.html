alexei Markin

Straight dark hair covering blue eyes, skinny physique, white embroidered traditional Russian shirt

Born in a small village in western Russia to a severely depressed woman with four children (Alexei the oldest, twins Lyuba and Nikolai, and Ania the youngest). When Nikolai died their mother went crazy with grief. Nearly 2 years later their house burned down (most likely set on fire by mother), killing both parents as well as Lyuba. Alexei escaped with Ania and went west, eventually ending up in Lithuania and begging fare on a cargo ship to Denmark.

His voice is starting to get lower and crack more often, but he still sounds like a kid.

He inherited clinical depression from his mother, which became very apparent after his family died and he left Russia. He has some guilt and trauma related to the fire- he was the oldest kid and most responsible, so he should have saved them, or shouldn't have run away.

Their parents loved each other, but their mother went crazy sometimes, causing their father to immerse himself in his work or focus his efforts on taking care of her, ignoring his children inadvertently. Alexei was in charge most of the time and felt very pressured. He's had some trouble adjusting to life on Seezo since he was older when he arrived, and he's no longer the sole responsible person around for Ania. The other boys try to get him to do things like swimming and hiking, but he's shy and nervous. Aidan especially has taken him under his wing, and he's actually come leaps and bounds since he arrived.

He celebrates his name day on June 10, but still doesn't like to make a big deal out of it. Aidan and Henry and the rest always plan something, however.

Ania is still his biggest motivation since he doesn't want her to experience the pain he's gone through. She can get him to come out of his shell the best of anyone. He's deeply grateful Min and everyone, especially Sabah, for taking care of her when he couldn't.
