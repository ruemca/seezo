alexander von Kleist

The only child of King Friedrich III of Germany and the Polish princess Izabella Maria.

He's restless, but can sit still for hours at his desk contemplating something.

He isn't a people person and has a hard time reading people's body language. He much prefers animals, who can't talk back, like his great white stallion, or his dogs. He broke the stallion himself, when he was 14 years old.

He likes everything to be meticulous, with everything in it's place and everything done the same way every time. He proudly carries on German traditions and traditions within the royal family. This makes him uncomfortable with Min as his wife- she isn't traditional in any way. He's confused how he feels about Min. He doesn't love her the way a husband should love a wife, but he cares for her in some deeper sense.
